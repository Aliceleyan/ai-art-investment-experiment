Step 1

Participant Consent / 参与同意

You are invited to take part in a study about how different information interfaces influence art investment decision-making. You will evaluate four fictional artwork investment opportunities. Some pages show conventional artwork information, while others show information organised through an AI-enabled knowledge filtering interface.

您将参加一项关于不同信息界面如何影响艺术品投资决策的研究。您需要评估四个虚构艺术品投资案例；部分页面展示传统艺术品资料，部分页面展示由 AI 知识过滤界面组织的信息。

Important / 重要说明： The AI interface does not provide investment advice, a final buy/sell recommendation, or a single correct price. It only organises and interprets decision-relevant knowledge for research purposes.
AI 界面不会提供投资建议、最终买卖推荐或唯一正确价格；它仅用于组织和解释与决策相关的知识。
