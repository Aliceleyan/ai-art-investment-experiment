Step 1

Participant Consent / 参与同意

You will review four fictional artwork investment cases. Each case gives you information that an investor might consider before deciding whether an artwork is worth buying.

您将阅读四个虚构艺术品投资案例。每个案例都会提供投资者在判断一件艺术品是否值得购买前可能会考虑的信息。

Participant role / 参与者角色： Please answer as someone who is interested in art and is willing to consider investing money in artworks. You do not need to be a professional art investor, but please imagine that you are evaluating each artwork as a potential investment opportunity.
请以“对艺术品感兴趣，并愿意考虑用资金投资艺术品”的身份作答。您不需要是专业艺术品投资者，但请想象自己正在评估每件艺术品是否值得购买。
Important / 重要说明： Some pages show the information as a regular artwork dossier. Other pages show the same information after an AI tool has organised it. The AI does not tell you whether to buy the artwork, and it does not give one correct price. It only helps identify which information may matter most, which information may be less important, and which points may still need expert judgement.
有些页面会以普通艺术品资料的方式展示信息；另一些页面会展示同样的信息，但经过 AI 工具整理。AI 不会告诉您是否应该购买，也不会给出唯一正确价格。它只帮助指出哪些信息可能更重要、哪些信息可能相对次要，以及哪些地方仍需要专家判断。

Please imagine that you are considering whether each fictional artwork is worth investing in. Review the information shown on each page and then answer the investment judgment questions.
